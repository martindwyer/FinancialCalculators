System framework, coding, testing.

System framework: We often use other reliable frameworks to save time and money. For web projects, we often utilize React or Next.js. For enterprise applications, Microsoft ASP.NET or Java Spring frameworks are frequent recommendations.

Coding:Our development process is designed to meet your project needs with iterative cycles of coding, testing and review. We start with one or two of your highest priority goals. Coding efforts initially are focused on these goals alone, allowing us to deliver quickly a functional application for your review.

Testing: Testing includes client review and approval of each set of goals as they are developed and implemented into the application. Once your highest priority goals are met, we continue the coding and testing process.
